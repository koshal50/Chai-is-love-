Our Heritage

Brewing Traditions, One Cup at a Time

Chai is more than just a beverage — it's a tradition passed down through generations, a moment of pause in our busy lives, and a bridge that connects hearts across tables. Our story began in the misty hills of Darjeeling, where our founders discovered not just the perfect blend of tea leaves, but a philosophy of life itself.

We believe that every cup tells a story. The warmth of the clay cup in your hands, the aromatic steam rising like morning mist, the first sip that awakens your senses — these are the moments we live for. Our mission is simple: to bring authentic chai culture to every corner of the world, preserving heritage while embracing innovation.

From selecting the finest tea leaves from organic plantations to perfecting the blend of spices that dance on your palate, we honor the craft of chai-making with reverence and passion. Each ingredient is carefully sourced, each blend thoughtfully created, and each cup made with love.
